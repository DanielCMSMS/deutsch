Modal Verbs

Modal verbs in English add more detail to verbs in a sentence. The words can, must, and would are examples of modal verbs:

I am eating lunch.
I can eat lunch.

I am studying for the test.
I must study for the test.

Like in English, modal verbs exist in German. Some examples are the words koennen, muessen, sollen, wollen, and duerfen. There are key differences on how modals are used in German.
